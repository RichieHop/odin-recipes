Air Fryer Sausage Rolls with Black Pudding

Description

Sausage rolls with a hint of black pudding.

Ingredients

  • 300g/10½oz sausage meat or sausages with skins removed
  • ½ tsp mustard powder
  • ½ tsp dried sage
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 100g/3½oz black pudding, casing removed, roughly chopped
  • 1 sheet (320g) ready-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten
  • Your favourite dipping sauce, to serve

Steps

  1. Using a fork, mix together the sausage meat, mustard powder, herbs and black pudding.
  2. Using your hands, mould the mixture into two long sausage shapes.
  3. Cut the pastry in half lengthways.
  4. Lay the meat mixture along the centre of each piece of pastry.
  5. Brush the edges with beaten egg, then fold the pastry over the filling and crimp the edges with a fork to seal.
  6. Cut into sausage rolls, to the size you prefer.
  7. Brush over the sausage rolls with beaten egg, then air-fry at 200C for 12 to 14 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot or cold, with your favourite dipping sauce.
