Couscous with Sundried Tomatoes

Description

Couscous with sundried tomatoes and salad.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups (425ml) chicken or vegetable stock (or water)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1½ cups couscous
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, roughly chopped and 1 tablespoon oil reserved
  • 1 cup roughly chopped pitted Kalamata olives
  • Diced cucumber (about a quarter)
  • Diced red pepper (about half)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the water (or stock), salt, butter, and oil to a boil.
  2. Stir in the couscous, cover tightly with a lid, and remove from heat.
  3. Let the couscous steam for 5 minutes.
  4. While the couscous is cooking, combine the tomatoes and their oil and olives in a large pan over medium high heat.
  5. Sauté for a few minutes until the mixture becomes fragrant, then stir in the couscous, cucumber and red pepper.
  6. Season with salt and pepper before serving.
